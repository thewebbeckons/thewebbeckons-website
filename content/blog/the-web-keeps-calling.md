# The web keeps calling

This has been a long time coming but I feel like I finally figured out how to finish this site, and that is to not finish it. Just to put it out there, it's not perfect, and it never will be. But I'm going to finally try not to worry about that and just work on it as I go. The web is a place that is constantly evolving, and maybe I should embrace that same model for this site and my own work. I always wanted to make something that was perfect, and I never actually finished because of that.

Hopefully I can keep this site alive and keep it evolving, and maybe even make it a place where I can share my thoughts and ideas with others. The web has always been beckoning me, and I think it's time I answer that call.
