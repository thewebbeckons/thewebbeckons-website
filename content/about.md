# About

I'm Jesse, a front-end developer based in Toronto, Canada.

I believe that great software should feel natural — like it was always meant to exist. My work sits at the intersection of design and engineering, where every pixel has a purpose and every interaction tells a story.

## What I Do

I specialize in building modern web applications with:

- **Nuxt & Vue** — Full-stack applications with server-side rendering
- **TypeScript** — Type-safe, maintainable code
- **Tailwind CSS** — Utility-first, responsive design
- **Figma** — Design systems and prototyping
- **Supabase** — Backend-as-a-service for rapid development

## Philosophy

I approach development with a few core principles:

- **Simplicity over complexity** — The best solution is often the simplest one
- **Performance is a feature** — Fast experiences keep users engaged
- **Accessibility by default** — The web should work for everyone
- **Design with intention** — Every detail matters

## Beyond the Screen

When I'm not coding, you'll find me exploring Toronto's coffee scene, reading about design history, or experimenting with new tools and frameworks.

Feel free to [reach out](/) if you'd like to chat about a project, collaboration, or just to say hello.
